SmartPath

SMARTPATH

Equipping students with the skills to thrive in an AI-driven world.

The SmartPath programme is a two-year workshop designed to introduce students to artificial intelligence and guide them towards sector-specific applications of AI in their future careers.

Year 1: Foundations in AI

  • Understanding what AI is and its evolution
  • Introduction to machine learning (ML) and its applications
  • Exploring large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT
  • Assessing the strengths and limitations of AI systems
  • Practicing effective prompting and tool usage
  • Examining ethics, bias, and AI regulations
  • Learning how AI is transforming industries
  • Developing personalized strategies for AI use

Year 2: Specialization

Students select a focus area to explore the impact and integration of AI within their chosen field, including:

  • Management & Consulting
  • Law
  • Business Studies
  • Data Analytics
  • Other emerging sectors
